About ARTPARK

ARTPARK @ IISc (AI & Robotics Technology Park) fosters innovations in AI & Robotics by bringing together the best of research, startup, industry, and government ecosystems.

We drive mission-oriented deep-tech projects, technology business incubation, and translational research in areas such as industrial automation, mobility, agriculture, healthcare and education.

ARTPARK is seed-funded by the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Govt. of India, under the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS) and the Govt. of Karnataka.

The Health Team at ARTPARK focuses on improving data and AI driven population health systems through the following arms:

Support Research Translation

Through our various collaborations with academia and government, we work to build the data and model infrastructure to enable translation into real-world applications.

Enable Innovations

Through supporting the development of new models in-house as proof-of-concepts, or building innovative modeling approaches and data systems.

Community of Practice

Support a community of practice in AI and data driven public health by making our datasets and systems available, and building our technical artifacts open source.

Resources

Access our comprehensive data catalogue and powerful technical artifacts to accelerate your research and innovation.
